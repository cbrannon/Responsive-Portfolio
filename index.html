About Me

Hi! My name is Cody Brannon. I'm an Atlanta native who enjoys music, video games, and coffee. I currently live in Cabbagetown with my fiancee, Erika, our cat, Mo, and dog, Obi. I'm a bit of a homebody who loves to read Fantasy/Sci-fi novels and play Overwatch. When I do get out I like to go see live music or local festivals around Atlanta. I currently work at Eaton's lighting division where I am a production Graphic Designer helping to develop marketing material for their Outdoor and Sports lighting product lines.
